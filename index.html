COFFEE TIMEE

Coffee With A Conscience

From seed to cup, Coffee Timee's Beans are grown, processed, and roasted to meet a premium coffee standard. Our goal is to create the finest possible roasts with world-class beans from all the major coffee-producing regions. All our beans are Fair Trade and many are Farm Gate certified, meaning that farmers are paid directly, "at the farm's gate," eliminating the middlemen. “When the coffee is produced and roasted sustainably, and the growers are compensated fairly, the coffee just tastes better.”
